A Community Map

Arcane Dimensions is the best showcase for what a community can do for a game. One map in particular really pushes even more modern source ports of Quake to its limits.

Tears of the False God

Tears of the False God has the player run around a large map and has multiple exits. This is largely due to its emphasis on a lore of a False God trying to overtake the "real" one in the real one's realm.

This is showcased by the map being split up into multiple "realms" that are actually cleverly all hosted on the same map. The map uses intermediate pathways to signal to the player when they are walking between realms. The most secret ones are locked away and require the player to collect the "tears" hidden around the map.

The movement between "realms" in this map is emphasized with the skybox changing throughout different areas. This is set when the player enters different teleporters as they traverse the level.
