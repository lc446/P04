Lucca's Maps

The Multiplayer Map: Cool Cubes

I like to think I have a few good ideas from time to time. This map is much smaller than others, but are aimed to execute one solid idea: Unorthodox use of the skybox.

What is not apparent in normal gameplay of Quake is that the skybox blocks any and all projectiles and hitscan weapons. Explosive projectiles in particular despawn and thus do not explode on impact. This map is littered with skybox blocks with contribute to some insane looking geometry but also serve the gameplay as you must be careful where you shoot your grenades and rockets.

In game, with multiple skybox brushes the map is rendered with a unique style I've come to appreciate.

The Singleplayer Map: Grape Time

This map's name is nonsense, so I didn't feel tied to any one idea when creating it. It ended up becoming a battle through 2 ships that is action packed.

These add fun gameplay elements that make the map much more interesting to play through than just shooting in corridors. The map even has rocket jumping elements, which older maps did not know about when designed.
